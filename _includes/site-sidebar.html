Brief About

Jihadology.net serves as a clearinghouse for jihādī primary source material, original analysis, and translation service. It is a personal project of Aaron Y. Zelin and is not associated with The Washington Institute for Near East Policy.

Docs.jihadology.net is a contribution made and maintained by David Clark. Learn more about it by visiting us on Github.
